Standards

 

AC-2.1 All passwords must have at least eight (8) characters.

 

VIEW EXCEPTIONS

 

AC-2.2 All passwords must include at least one of each of the following: upper case, lower case, and non-alphabetic characters.

 

Guideline: For example, bp45Y7g6

 

AC-2.3 Password history must be a minimum of eight (8) passwords; Password history is a security setting that determines the number of unique passwords that have to be associated with a user account before the old password can be reused.

 

AC-2.4 Passwords must have a minimum age of three (3) days.

 

AC-2.5 Passwords must be configured to require change at next login when reset by someone other than the end user, including automated processes

 

AC-2.6 Applications and systems must require users to change their passwords at least every ninety (90) days

 

AC-2.7 The number of consecutive attempts to enter an incorrect password must be limited to five (5) unsuccessful attempts, after which the User ID must be automatically locked until unlocked by a specific and authorized user initiated action

 

AC-2.8 All passwords must be promptly changed if they are suspected of being disclosed, or are known to have been disclosed, to unauthorized parties
