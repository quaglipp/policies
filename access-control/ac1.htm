Warning Banners (AC-1)

Control Objective

Official company legal-approved warning banners must be displayed before users connect to company (or company-branded) applications or systems

Controls

AC-3.1

Warning Banners
Warning banners must display the following verbiage:

This system is solely for the use of authorized company employees and contractors. Company reserves the right at any time to monitor usage of this system to ensure compliance with all applicable company policies and federal regulations. Your use of this system constitutes your consent to monitoring by company, and your express agreement to use this system in compliance with applicable laws. Any unauthorized use of or access to this system may result in a revocation of your user privileges, other disciplinary action up to and including termination of employment or contracts, or referrals to law enforcement officials including the provision of evidence of any unauthorized us or access to law enforcement.

Guideline: Banner text should be in an easily readable font and consider visually impaired users.
