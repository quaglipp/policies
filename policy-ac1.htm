Standards

 

AC-1.1 Warning banners must display the following verbiage:

 

"This system is solely for the use of authorized Comcast employees and contractors. Comcast reserves the right at any time to monitor usage of this system to ensure compliance with all applicable Comcast policies and federal regulations. Your use of this system constitutes your consent to monitoring by Comcast, and your express agreement to use this system in compliance with applicable laws. Any unauthorized use of or access to this system may result in a revocation of your user privileges, other disciplinary action up to and including termination of employment or contracts, or referrals to law enforcement officials including the provision of evidence of any unauthorized us or access to law enforcement."

 

Guideline: Banner text should be in an easily readable font and consider visually impaired users
